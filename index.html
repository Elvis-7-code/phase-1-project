Bruschetta Recipe

  1. Chop fresh tomatoes and basil.
  2. Mix with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toast bread slices.
  4. Top with the tomato mixture and serve.

Filet Mignon Recipe

  1. Season filet with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear in a hot pan with butter for 3-4 mins per side.
  3. Finish in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for 10 mins.
  4. Serve with mashed potatoes and red wine sauce.

Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe

  1. Melt chocolate and butter together.
  2. Mix with sugar, eggs and flour
  3. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 12 mins.
  4. Serve warm with ice cream
